About Us

Welcome to Perfume paradise! We offer a wide range of exquisite fragrances for both men and women.

The idea of ​​this project is considered a pioneering and new idea, which is to allow customers to create their own perfume on our behalf, in addition to the perfumes that the store features.

The owners of this project are a student and a university doctor from Al-Khwarizmi Technical University College, and this site was developed in HTML and CSS.

The idea for this store began when Dr. Magdalene Al-Sabbagh asked student Abdul Rahman Salah Khalil to submit an entrepreneurial project

The idea of ​​the project is not considered a new idea, but every day we develop ourselves more and aspire to expand to all countries of the world and for our name to become one of the first in the world of perfumes.
